    Media Lab

    Availability of Machines and Devices

    From time to time, certain machines and equipment may not be available for use due to renovations, expansions, maintenance, repairs or other similar reasons. This may be because they are out of order or require initial commissioning. If you require the use of specific equipment for your project, we ask that you contact the relevant contacts by email or telephone in advance to ensure availability.

    We understand the importance of professionally documenting and showcasing your projects. That's why we have set up a media lab that provides you with all the necessary tools and software to take impressive product photos, create exciting podcasts, record lectures, or produce convincing pitch videos.

    Who is the Media Lab contact?

    Tim Höink

    "Hey, I'm Tim and I'm responsible for the Media Lab in the Makerspace. So if you want to work with our audio systems, our cameras or our event equipment, then I'm your contact person. As I am also active in many other areas of the Makerspace, you are more than welcome to contact me about other topics related to your project. In addition to specific technical questions about the implementation of your ideas, I am also happy to help you with questions about the methods of design thinking."

    Here's What You Can Do in the Media Lab

    • Capture high-quality photos and videos using our DSLR and system cameras
    • Record crystal-clear audio with our professional-grade equipment
    • Create immersive 360° photos and videos
    • Broadcast live videos
    • Drone footage (not yet operational at this time)
    • Plus, you can edit and polish your work using our extensive range of software for sound, photo, and video editing

    Under certain conditions, most of the equipment in the media lab is also available for borrowing. Just ask us for details!

    How You Can Use the Media Lab

    To work in the media lab, you must first attend the General Safety Instruction and then receive instruction for the respective application (e.g. product photos or 360° camera). After that, you are allowed to work independently with most of the equipment. Exceptions are drones and camera crane, which have more requirements. Please contact us separately.

    If you want to use the media lab, simply let us know on-site or contact us.

    To the "Media Lab" work folder with further documents

    In this folder you will find further documents about the media lab such as user manuals, operating instructions, tutorials and other things to make your work easier.
    As always: Together we are stronger - we are happy about every [contribution to the collection of knowledge and experience]!(feedback.en.md)

    Photo and Video Cameras

    Our photo studio offers a wide selection of SLR cameras that are capable of recording videos as well. We also provide professional accessories such as different lenses, tripods, a light tent, photo flashes with a vast selection of light shapers, light meters, and even a camera crane.

    Nowadays, 360° content is becoming increasingly popular in various areas such as home tours, exploratory tours, product marketing, and immersive virtual reality videos.
